6 days 5 nights to explore the Masai Mara National Reserve, Lake Elementaita, Amboseli and Tsavo East


ITINERARY


Day 1 : Nairobi - Masai Mara

We will pick you up from the airport or your accommodation in Nairobi generally around 7:00 a.m. but it will depend on where you will be in the city or at the airport. Departure from Nairobi towards Masai Mara passing by the Rift Valley viewpoint where we will stop to take some photos, near Suswa Ranges and Mount Longonot. Afterwards we will continue on the road to the national reserve of Masai Mara, arrival at the lodge or camp in the afternoon, settling in, lunch and a short rest and then a safari after 3:45 p.m. when the sun goes down and the wildlife emerges from the shadows. At 6:00 p.m. Return to the lodge or camp where you can take a hot water shower before dinner. Accommodation will be at lodge or camp on full board basis.

Day 2 : Full day safari drive

Breakfast at 6:30 a.m. and departure for a full day at Kenya's most popular sanctuary where we will have the best opportunity to spot the Big Five (lion, leopard, buffalo, rhino and elephant) along the savannah plains of the Masai Mara reserve. With the visit to the Mara River where you will be able to see hippos in the ponds and crocodiles sunbathing on the banks of the river. At 1:00 p.m., I will take you to a place for a picnic which will have been prepared by your camp leader. After a short rest. you will resume the safari. Return to camp at the end of the day. You will have the opportunity to visit a local Maasai village. Note that tips are given to the Masai when you visit their village. After a hot shower, a good dinner awaits you. Accommodation will be at the lodge or camp on a full board basis.

Day 3 : Masai Mara -Elementaita

Breakfast and drive to Lake Elementaita, arrival at the lodge for lunch, free day to discover the lake's wildlife. Dinner and night at the Lodge full board. Among the many lakes in the Rift Valley, nature lovers will particularly appreciate Lake Elementaita. The lake is surrounded by rich vegetation – acacia forests – where many species of birds have been found here

Day 4 : Lake Elementaita-Amboseli Park

Departure in the morning after breakfast, drive towards Amboseli national park, lunch Installation at the lodge then rest and safaris around 4 p.m. at Amboseli park and return to the lodge for dinner and overnight, Lodge or Camp accommodation , full board.

Day 5 : Amboseli-Tsavo East National Park

Departure after breakfast, safari in Amboseli National Park and leave the park in the direction of Tsavo East. The semi-arid plains of East Tsavo extend from the Taita Hills to the Yatta escarpment where the broad Galana River flows and where large numbers of hippos and crocodiles live. Herds of elephants as well as herds of buffalo are numerous on the vast plains of Tsavo-East. Tsavo East also has the particularity of hosting numerous groups of lions, cheetahs also like the plains of Tsavo East. safari in Tsavo East park then lunch and rest at the lodge and do another safari around 4 p.m. and return to the lodge for dinner and overnight. Tsavo East National Park is one of the oldest parks in Kenya. The two national parks Tsavo East and Tsavo West together form one of the largest nature reserves in Africa (21,000 km2). Tsavo as a whole has an area of ​​10 million hectares, within which there are savannah, mountain ranges and hills, acacia forests and an extensive hydrographic network. Accommodation Voi Safari Lodge, full board.

Day 6 : Tsavo East-Diani/Mombasa/Watamu

Morning after breakfast game drives in Tsavo East National Park in search of lions, cheetahs, antelopes, elephants with many other animals and leave the park for the direction of Nairobi airport or to your Mombasa hotel… end of safari.
