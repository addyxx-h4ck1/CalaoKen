7 days safari Masai Mara, Lake Nakuru, Amboseli, Tsavo West and Tsavo East from Nairobi.


ITINERARY


Day 1 : Nairobi - Masai Mara

We will pick you up from the airport or your accommodation in Nairobi generally around 7:00 a.m. but it will depend on where you will be in the city or at the airport. Departure from Nairobi towards Masai Mara passing by the Rift Valley viewpoint where we will stop to take some photos, near Suswa Ranges and Mount Longonot. Afterwards we will continue on the road to the national reserve of Masai Mara, arrival at the lodge or camp in the afternoon, settling in, lunch and a short rest and then a safari after 3:45 p.m. when the sun goes down and the wildlife emerges from the shadows. At 6:00 p.m. Return to the lodge or camp where you can take a hot water shower before dinner. Accommodation will be at lodge or camp on full board basis.

Day 2 : Full day safari drive

Breakfast at 6:30 a.m. and departure for a full day at Kenya's most popular sanctuary where we will have the best opportunity to spot the Big Five (lion, leopard, buffalo, rhino and elephant) along the savannah plains of the Masai Mara reserve. With the visit to the Mara River where you will be able to see hippos in the ponds and crocodiles sunbathing on the banks of the river. At 1:00 p.m., I will take you to a place for a picnic which will have been prepared by your camp leader. After a short rest. you will resume the safari. Return to camp at the end of the day. You will have the opportunity to visit a local Maasai village. Note that tips are given to the Masai when you visit their village. After a hot shower, a good dinner awaits you. Accommodation will be at the lodge or camp on a full board basis.

Day 3 : Masai Mara-Lake Nakuru

After breakfast, drive to Lake Nakuru National Reserve safari in the reserve to the Lodge or Camp, installation and lunch and then afternoon safari then return to the Lodge for dinner and overnight. Full board accommodation.

Day 4 : Nakuru-Amboseli

Departure in the morning after breakfast, drive towards Amboseli national park, lunch Installation at lodge or camp, lunch then rest and safaris around 4 p.m. at Amboseli park and return to lodge or camp for dinner and overnight .

Day 5 : Amboseli – Tsavo West National Park

Departure after breakfast for Tsavo West safari to Tsavo West National Park with a visit to Mzima Spring pass; resurgence of crystalline rivers filtered in the volcanic rocks of Tsavo, forming a body of water inhabited by hippos and crocodiles, arrive at the Lodge for lunch and rest until 4 p.m. for an afternoon safari until 18 then return to the lodge for the night.

Day 6 : Tsavo West-Tsavo East.

Departure after breakfast for Tsavo East. The semi-arid plains of East Tsavo extend from the Taita Hills to the Yatta escarpment where the broad Galana River flows and where large numbers of hippos and crocodiles live. Herds of elephants as well as herds of buffalo are numerous on the vast plains of Tsavo-East. Tsavo East also has the particularity of hosting numerous groups of lions, cheetahs also like the plains of Tsavo East. safari in the Tsavo East park, then go to the lodge for installation, lunch and rest until 4 p.m. then afternoon safari until 6 p.m. and return to the lodge for the night: Tsavo East national park is one of the oldest parks in Kenya. The two national parks Tsavo East and Tsavo West together form one of the largest nature reserves in Africa (21,000 km2). Tsavo as a whole has an area of ​​10 million hectares, within which there are savannah, mountain ranges and hills, acacia forests and an extensive hydrographic network.

Day 7 : Tsavo East-Mombasa/Diani/Watamu

Morning after breakfast game drives in Tsavo East National Park in search of lions, cheetah, antelopes, elephants with many other animals and leave the park for the direction of Mombasa with lunch en route. Then continue the journey to your hotel in Mombasa/Diani or Watamu arriving in the afternoon…end of safari.

Day 8: Amboseli National Park - Tsavo West National Park

Departure after breakfast for Tsavo West safari to Tsavo West National Park with a visit to Mzima Spring pass; resurgence of crystalline rivers filtered in the volcanic rocks of Tsavo, forming a body of water inhabited by hippos and crocodiles, arrival at Ngulia safari Lodge for lunch and rest until 4 p.m. for an afternoon safari until 'at 6 then return for dinner and overnight at the Lodge. Accommodation: lodge on full board basis.
