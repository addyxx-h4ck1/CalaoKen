Masai Mara National Reserve Safari from Nairobi


ITINERARY


Day 1 : Nairobi - Masai Mara

We will pick you up from the airport or your accommodation in Nairobi generally around the morning but it will depend on where you will be in the city or at the airport. Departure from Nairobi towards Masai Mara passing by the Rift Valley viewpoint where we will stop to take some photos, near Suswa Ranges and Mount Longonot. Afterwards we will continue on the road to the national reserve of Masai Mara, arrival at the lodge or Camp in the afternoon for installation, lunch and a short rest and then a safari after 3:45 p.m. when the sun goes down and the wildlife emerges from the shadows. At 6:00 p.m. Return to camp where we can take a hot water shower before dinner. Accommodation at the Camp or Lodge on a full board basis.

Day 2 : Masai Mara Full day

Breakfast at 6:30 a.m. and departure for a full day to Kenya's most popular sanctuary where you will have the best opportunity to spot the Big Five (lion, leopard, buffalo, rhino and elephant) along the savannah plains of the Masai reserve Marah. With a visit to the Mara River where you will be able to see hippos in the ponds and crocodiles sunbathing on the banks of the river. At 1:00 p.m., we will take you to a place for a picnic which will have been prepared by your camp leader. After a short rest. you will resume the safari. Return to camp at the end of the day. After a hot shower, a good dinner awaits you. Accommodation at the Camp or Lodge on a full board basis.

Day 3 : Masai Mara - Nairobi

n the morning after breakfast safari in the reserve and we will have the opportunity to visit a local Masai village (optional). Note that tips are given to the Masai when you visit their village, and leave Masai Mara for Nairobi at your hotel or airport, end of safari.
