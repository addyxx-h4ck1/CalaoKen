Safari 2 days 1 night from Mombasa, Diani or Watamu


ITINERARY


Day 1 : Diani/Mombasa/Watamu - Tsavo East National Park

Departure from your hotel in Mombasa/Diani or Malindi, take the road to reach the Tsavo East National Park with your French-speaking guide, in 4×4 or Mini-ubs with opening roof. The landscape of Tsavo East is a plain with red earth. You will see the baobabs and animals up close such as the “red” elephants. Safari en route to the lodge where you will arrive around noon. Installation at the Lodge and lunch. Rest then start an afternoon safari around 4 p.m., until sunset. Dinner and Night at Au Sentrim Tsavo or similar on full board basis Tsavo East National Park is one of the oldest parks in Kenya. The two national parks Tsavo East and Tsavo West together form one of the largest nature reserves in Africa (21,000 km2). Tsavo as a whole has an area of ​​10 million hectares, within which there are savannah, mountain ranges and hills, acacia forests and an extensive hydrographic network.

Day 2 : Tsavo East National Park - Mombasa/ Diani/ Watamu

Morning after breakfast game drives in Tsavo East National Park in search of lions, cheetahs, antelopes, elephants with many other animals and leave the park for the direction of your hotel in Mombasa/Diani or Watamu arrived in the afternoon…end of safari.
