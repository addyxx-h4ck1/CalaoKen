Tsavo East/Tsavo West and Amboseli from Mombasa


ITINERARY


Day 1 : Mombasa/Diani-Tsavo East

Departure from your Mombasa/Diani hotel around 60:30, take the Mombasa-Nairobi highway for the Tsavo East National Park with your French-speaking driver guide, in a mini open-top bus: crossing the Bac then taking the Transafricaine to reach the entrance to the park around 10 a.m. The landscape of Tsavo East is a plain with red earth. You will see the baobabs and animals up close ls than the “red” elephants. Safari en route to the lodge where you will arrive around noon. Installation at the Lodge or Camp and lunch. Rest then mini safari around 4 p.m., until sunset. Dinner and night at the lodge or camp with full board.

Day 2 : Tsavo East-Amboseli

Very early in the morning, after breakfast, go out and enjoy a morning safari, Leave it in the direction of Amboseli National Park, Arrival at the Camp or lodge check inn lunch. At 3:30 p.m., we will take you to Masai village for a visit (optional). Enjoy a safari in this Kilimanjaro land where predators like lions and cheetahs are often spotted. Gnus, Thomson's gazelles, elephants, giraffes and many more wild animals on the plains. Return to the lodge camp for Dinner and overnight.

Day 3 : Amboseli – Diani

After breakfast, head out of the camp or lodge and start the day where the sunrise gives the photographer an excellent shot of Kilimanjaro. Safari in Amboseli Park and then leave Amboseli through the gate, join the highway back to your hotel with lunch en route arriving in Mombasa at the end of the day, End of safari. Similar routes
