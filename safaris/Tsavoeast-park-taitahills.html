Tsavo East Park and Taita Hills National Reserve, Diani


ITINERARY


Day 1 : Diani, Mombasa, Watamu - Taita Hills Private Game Reserve

Departure 6:00 a.m. from your hotel for the Taita Hills private reserve arrive and do first safari in the unique reserve, then return for lunch at Taita Hills game Lodge, after lunch short rest and then a safari in the unique Taita reserve Hills until evening then continue to Sarova Salt Lick game lodge, built in a modified manner for guests to enjoy viewing the animals in the reserve, elephants, lions, cheetahs, impalas etc. dinner and overnight at Salt Lick game lodge.

Day 2 : Taita Hills National Reserve - At Tsavo East National Park

Departure for a morning safari from 6:30 a.m. to 9:00 a.m. return to the lodge for breakfast, after breakfast departure for Tsavo East National Park. The landscape of Tsavo East is a plain with red earth. You will see the baobabs and animals up close such as the “red” elephants. Safari en route to the lodge where you will arrive around noon. Installation at the lodge and lunch, rest then start a mini safari after around 4 p.m., until sunset and return to the lodge for Dinner and Night. Accommodation: Voi Safari Lodge Tsavo East National Park is one of the oldest parks in Kenya. The two national parks Tsavo East and Tsavo West together form one of the largest nature reserves in Africa (21,000 km2). Tsavo as a whole has an area of ​​10 million hectares, within which there are savannah, mountain ranges and hills, acacia forests and an extensive hydrographic network.

Day 3 : Tsavo East National Park - Diani, Mombasa or Watamu

Morning after breakfast safaris in Tsavo East National Park in search of lions, cheetahs, antelopes, elephants with many other animals and leave for the direction of Mombasa arriving in the afternoon around 2:00 p.m.…end safari
